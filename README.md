# 🛡️ CraftShield

### Creator Protection Platform for Artisans

CraftShield is a platform designed to protect artisans, jewellery designers, and creative entrepreneurs throughout the custom-order lifecycle. It provides a secure and transparent workflow that minimizes financial risks, protects original designs, and builds trust between buyers and sellers.

---

## 🎯 Problem

Custom orders are often managed through informal messaging platforms, leading to:

* Order cancellations after production begins
* Unpaid work due to lack of advance payments
* No formal agreements between buyers and sellers
* Design plagiarism and lack of ownership proof
* Limited order transparency and dispute evidence

---

## 💡 Solution

CraftShield introduces a secure custom-order workflow:

```text
Register
    ↓
Browse / Custom Request
    ↓
Seller Confirmation
    ↓
Digital Contract
    ↓
Advance Payment
    ↓
Design Protection
    ↓
Production Tracking
    ↓
Final Payment
    ↓
Delivery
    ↓
Reviews & Digital Records
```

The platform combines authentication, order management, digital agreements, design protection, payment tracking, and dispute support into a single ecosystem.
